   a. process_creation.c (Exercise 1: Using fork() in C)

2. Job of the Linker:
   Combines object files (.o) and libs into an executable. Resolves symbols (e.g., links hello() call to definition) and relocates addresses for memory.

3. Job of the Loader:
   OS loads executable into memory, sets up environment (stack, heap), loads dynamic libs, and jumps to main(). Handles runtime deps like printf.
